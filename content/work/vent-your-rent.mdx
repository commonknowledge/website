[Generation Rent](https://www.generationrent.org/) campaigns for safe, fair and secure homes for renters.

We worked together on a tool that allows people to explore what the renting crisis looks like in their area, add their own experiences of private renting and sign the [National Renters Manifesto](https://www.rentermanifesto.org/read_the_manifesto_full).
